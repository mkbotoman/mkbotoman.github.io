About Me

I am a Salt Lake City based software developer and entreprenuer. I consider myself creative, though I don't have much evidence that that is the case. I love making new things, from pies to projects. I appreciate community envolvement and strive to be a active member of the Salt Lake community and the code community.

I recently decided to pursue my MBA at the University of Utah to gain a better understanding of what it takes to build a company from the gound up. I want to be able to not just create value through the code I write, but also through strategic vision, creatvity and passion for the projects I am working on.

I have a strong passion for nature and sustainability. I studied Envivornmental Science in my undergraduate and began my career in the building energy efficiency area. I would like to create projects that change the way people think about conservation and the environment. I would like to share my own passion for trails, mountains, lakes and deserts with the world.
